Basic Starting Stitches

Crocheting offers a spectrum of stitches, from the simple single crochet to the intricate waffle stitch. Beginners often start with the single crochet, a basic yet versatile stitch. As skills grow, crocheters may delve into more complex stitches like the waffle stitch, which creates a textured, grid-like pattern. With practice and patience, crocheters can explore a myriad of stitches, each adding its own charm to their creations.
